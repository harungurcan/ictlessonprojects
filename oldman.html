Old Man: A Poem by Chris Roe

By your side 
There is peace, 
A quiet place 
Of beauty and realisation, 
Where wisdom 
Seeks reason and understanding, 
Where myth is laid to rest 
By the reality of knowledge. 
By your side there is hope 
That arrogance and greed 
Will not destroy, 
That selfless love 
Will prevail. 
By your side, 
Life is sacred. 
By your side, 
A future 
For the children. 
By your side 
There is love.
